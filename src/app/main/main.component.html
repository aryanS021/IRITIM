About IRITM

The Indian Railways Institute of Transport Management (IRITM) has been set up by the Minstry of Railways in August 1999 as a centralized training institute with the objective of imparting a a focused training to the officer of the Traffic Department of Indian Railways, The objetive was to bring about an attitudinal change in the young officers, orienting them for a positive response to Railway's transformation from a production-oriented to a customer-oriented organization in the years following the economic liberalization.

IRITM started functioning in August 1999 under the supervision of ED/PT/RDSO from a camp office located in RDSO. In August 2000, Railway Board sanctioned the operation of the post of Director , IRITM, Professor (Training), 2 post in Group B and 7 posts in Group C&D

The Hon'ble Prime Minster, Shri Atal Behari Vajpayee, laid the foundation stone of the IRITM campus on 31th January 2001 and also dedicated the Institute to the Nation on 21st May 2003
